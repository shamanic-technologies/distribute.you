Questions

Common questions

How does billing work exactly?
You buy credits upfront. Each credit covers one contact: we find them, write their email, and send it. Credits are consumed when an email reaches an inbox. Invalid addresses and hard bounces are filtered before sending and don't count against your balance.
Is there a free trial?
New accounts start with 50 free emails, no card required. That's enough to run a small campaign and see real opens and replies before spending anything.
Do credits expire?
Credits are valid for 12 months from purchase. Each new credit pack gets its own 12-month window. No auto-renew, no surprise charges.
Can I top up mid-campaign?
Yes. Buy more credits from your dashboard at any time. They're added instantly and active campaigns continue without interruption.
Do you need access to my domain or inbox?
No. Distribute uses its own pre-warmed sending infrastructure. You don't need to touch your domain or connect any mailbox to get started.
Are the emails GDPR and CAN-SPAM compliant?
Every email includes a working opt-out. Unsubscribes are processed automatically and the contact is permanently suppressed across all future campaigns. You remain the sender of record and are responsible for targeting prospects with a legitimate business purpose.
