Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

Distribute ("we", "our", "us") operates the website distribute.you and the Distribute outreach platform. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Contact: hello@distribute.you

2. Data we collect

Account data

When you create an account: your email address, name, and payment information (processed by Stripe — we do not store card details).

Campaign data

Product URLs, ICP descriptions, and campaign settings you provide when setting up outreach campaigns.

Usage data

Pages visited, features used, and interactions with the platform. Collected via server logs and first-party analytics.

Prospect data

Distribute sources prospect contact information (business email addresses, job titles, company names) from public databases and web scraping. This data is used solely to run your campaigns and is not sold to third parties.

3. How we use your data

  • To operate and improve the Distribute platform
  • To process payments and manage your account
  • To send transactional emails (campaign summaries, billing receipts)
  • To source and contact prospects on your behalf as instructed
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

4. Data sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the platform:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Postmark — transactional email delivery
  • Neon — database infrastructure
  • Railway — application hosting

All providers are bound by data processing agreements. No data is shared with advertisers or marketing platforms.

5. Prospect data and cold email compliance

Distribute sends cold email on your behalf. You are the sender of record. You are responsible for ensuring your campaigns comply with applicable laws including CAN-SPAM (US), CASL (Canada), and GDPR (EU/UK).

Every email sent through Distribute includes an unsubscribe mechanism. Unsubscribe requests are processed within 10 business days and the contact is suppressed from future campaigns.

6. Data retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your account plus 90 days after deletion. Campaign data (emails sent, replies received) is retained for 12 months then deleted unless you request earlier deletion. Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by law.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, restrict or object to processing, and request data portability. To exercise these rights, email hello@distribute.you.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some platform features may not function correctly.

9. Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel. We conduct periodic security reviews.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated by email to account holders at least 14 days before taking effect. Continued use of the platform after that date constitutes acceptance.
