Is cold email effective for marketing and creative agencies?

Yes. Agencies are one of the best use cases for cold email. Your ideal client is easy to profile (company size, industry, marketing maturity), the ticket size is large, and a single new retainer typically generates 10–50x the cost of a campaign. distribute sends from our own domains, so your brand reputation is never at risk.

What types of B2B service providers use distribute?

Consulting firms, IT managed services, accounting, legal, HR, PR, web development agencies, and really any business selling a service B2B with a ticket size above $5k. If you can describe who you want to reach (industry, company size, job title), distribute can find them.

Do I need to set up email domains or inboxes?

No. distribute handles every part of the sending infrastructure: domain registration, DNS setup, SPF / DKIM / DMARC configuration, inbox warming, and deliverability monitoring. You never touch an email account. Qualified replies are forwarded directly to your Gmail.
