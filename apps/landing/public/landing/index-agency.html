Common questions

Does distribute work for agencies that manage client campaigns?

Yes. You can run multiple campaigns from one account, one per client. Each has its own URL, budget, audience, and dashboard. You control the spend and see results separately for each client.

What kind of emails does distribute write?

Personalized cold emails based on your site and the prospect's company. We research each contact before writing. Subject lines and copy are tested and optimized over time based on reply rates.

How does the $25 match work?

Add a card, drop your URL, and start. For every $1 you spend we credit $1 back, up to $25, so your first $25 is matched. After that you pay as you go, at whatever your outcomes cost.

What happens when a prospect replies?

AI reads the reply and classifies it. If the person is interested, we forward the full conversation to your Gmail inbox with a "buyer" label. You reply directly. Unsubscribes and out-of-office messages never reach you.

Can I stop at any time?

Yes. Pause or stop any campaign immediately. Any unused budget stays in your account for future campaigns.

Do you handle deliverability and email warm-up?

We handle everything. Domain setup, email warm-up, sending infrastructure, bounce management, and deliverability monitoring. You don't need to touch any of it.

How does per-outcome pricing work?

You never pay per seat or per email. You set a daily budget and choose the outcome you want: a click, a meeting, or a signup. We spend the budget getting that outcome as cheaply as we can, and your real cost per outcome shows up live as the campaign runs.

What is the smallest budget I can start with?

One dollar a day. No minimum contract, no retainer. Raise it, lower it, or pause it whenever you want.

Do you send from my domain?

No. Every message goes out from our own agency inboxes and accounts, so your domain reputation is never at risk and there is nothing to warm up. We pitch your service to prospects on your behalf, and forward the interested ones to your Gmail.

Is distribute only cold email?

Cold email is where we run today because it is the most scalable, cheapest, and most profitable channel right now. We are channel agnostic underneath: ads, calls, and social come next, always aimed at handing you the lead at the lowest cost.
