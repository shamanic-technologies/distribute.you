Questions

Common questions

Does distribute work for agencies that manage client campaigns?
Yes. You can run multiple campaigns from one account, one per client. Each has its own URL, budget, audience, and dashboard. You control the spend and see results separately for each client.
What kind of emails does distribute write?
Personalized cold emails based on your site and the prospect's company. We research each contact before writing. Subject lines and copy are tested and optimized over time based on reply rates.
How does the 50 free emails trial work?
Sign up, drop your URL, and we send your first 50 emails for free. No credit card required. You see real results before spending anything. After that, you pay as you go — ~$90/meeting avg.
What happens when a prospect replies?
AI reads the reply and classifies it. If the person is interested, we forward the full conversation to your Gmail inbox with a "buyer" label. You reply directly. Unsubscribes and out-of-office messages never reach you.
Can I stop at any time?
Yes. Pause or stop any campaign immediately. Any unused budget stays in your account for future campaigns.
Do you handle deliverability and email warm-up?
We handle everything. Domain setup, email warm-up, sending infrastructure, bounce management, and deliverability monitoring. You don't need to touch any of it.
