FAQ

Technical questions

What domains do you send from?
We provision sending domains and manage all deliverability: setup, warm-up, DKIM/SPF/DMARC, rotation, and reputation monitoring. Replies are forwarded to your Gmail inbox. You never touch the sending infrastructure.
How does AI reply classification work?
Every reply gets processed by our classifier. Categories: buyer (interested, wants a call, asks a question), unsubscribe, bounce, out-of-office, and neutral. Only "buyer" replies get forwarded to your inbox with full conversation context and the prospect's contact data.
Can I run campaigns for multiple clients from one account?
Yes. Each campaign is isolated: its own URL, audience, budget, copy, and dashboard. You see all campaigns from your account. Billing is per campaign. You can pause or stop individual campaigns without affecting others.
What's the minimum viable budget for results?
$10/day is the minimum. At $10/day (~140 contacts), expect 1-3 qualified replies per week depending on your offer and audience. Most agencies run $30-100/day per client campaign for consistent pipeline.
How does prospecting work?
We derive your ICP from your site URL and targeting brief. We source contacts from multiple databases, verify them, and enrich each one before writing personalized emails. You do not provide a prospect list.
Is there an API?
API access is in private beta. If you need API access for CRM sync, reporting, or campaign management at scale, contact us. We prioritize agencies managing 3+ concurrent client campaigns.
