Before you launch

Questions serious buyers should ask.

What is distribute, in one sentence?

distribute is done-for-you B2B cold email outreach. Give it your website, confirm who you want to reach, choose the campaign objective, and set a daily limit. distribute handles prospecting, research, writing, sending, follow-ups, reply filtering, and reporting.

Is this just another cold email tool?

No. A cold email tool gives you software to operate. distribute runs the campaign on your behalf. You review the audience and angle, then handle the interested conversations.

How does pricing work?

You choose the result the campaign should pursue and set a maximum daily budget. distribute runs the campaign and shows the actual cost per measured visit, positive reply, or signup. Rates vary by market and campaign. There is no guaranteed fixed result price.

Can I really set this up in 30 seconds?

The basic setup is designed to take only a few minutes: add your website, review the proposed audience and campaign angle, choose the objective, and set the daily limit. Actual sending can still depend on review, compliance checks, workflow availability, and campaign capacity.

Does distribute send from my company domain?

No. Campaign messages are sent through inboxes managed by distribute or its partners. Your primary company domain does not need to be connected or warmed. Delivery and inbox placement are still not guaranteed.

Can I review the campaign before it sends?

You can review and adjust the proposed target audience and campaign direction before launch. Approval settings for generated content can vary by workflow and configuration.

What reaches my inbox?

Replies are classified by intent. Positive interest can be forwarded with the relevant contact and conversation context. Out-of-office replies, clear rejections, bounces, and opt-outs are handled inside the campaign workflow. Classification can make mistakes, so the dashboard remains the complete record.

Who owns and can export the campaign data?

You retain ownership of the data you submit. Campaign records available in your account can be exported for CRM use and lawful follow-up. Data retention after account termination is governed by the current Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Is cold outreach automatically GDPR or CAN-SPAM compliant?

No platform can make every campaign lawful by default. distribute handles working opt-outs and suppression workflows, but you remain responsible for lawful targeting, a valid business purpose or other legal basis, truthful claims, and the rules that apply in each recipient's jurisdiction.

Are results guaranteed?

No. Market size, offer quality, targeting, geography, copy, deliverability, website conversion, and sales execution all affect results. distribute publishes performance data so you can evaluate real economics rather than rely on a promise.

How do the welcome credits work?

Eligible new accounts may receive promotional credits that offset usage charges, currently advertised up to $25. Credits have no cash value and can be subject to eligibility, expiry, anti-abuse rules, and changing promotional terms.

Can agencies run campaigns for clients?

Yes. Separate campaigns can use different URLs, audiences, budgets, and reporting. Review the current account permissions and any resale or white-label restrictions in the Terms before offering the service to clients.
