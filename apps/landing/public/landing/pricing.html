Common questions

How does billing work?

You set a daily budget and add a card, like Google Ads. We run the outreach and you pay for outcomes after they're delivered, never upfront. We charge your card when your accrued spend reaches a billing threshold, or at the end of the month if you haven't reached it.

Do I get free credits?

Yes. We match your first $25 of spend dollar for dollar. The $25 in credits lands when you make your first payment, so your first campaigns effectively cost half.

When am I charged?

After outcomes are delivered. We bill your card when spend hits your current threshold, which starts small and rises to $50, then $200, then $500 as your account builds history, or on the last day of the month if you're still under it, whichever comes first.

Can I change my budget or pause?

Yes. Adjust your daily budget or pause anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately and you only ever pay for outcomes already delivered.

Do you need access to my domain or inbox?

No. Distribute uses its own pre-warmed sending infrastructure. You don't need to touch your domain or connect any mailbox to get started.

Are the emails GDPR and CAN-SPAM compliant?

Every email includes a working opt-out. Unsubscribes are processed automatically and the contact is permanently suppressed across all future campaigns. You remain the sender of record and are responsible for targeting prospects with a legitimate business purpose.
