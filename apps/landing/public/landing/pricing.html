Common questions

How does billing work exactly?

You buy credits upfront. Each credit covers one contact: we find them, write their email, and send it. Credits are used when an email reaches an inbox. Invalid addresses and hard bounces are filtered before sending and don't count against your balance.

Is there a free trial?

For every $1 you spend, we credit $1 back, up to $25. Your first $25 is matched dollar for dollar. A small campaign effectively costs half.

Do credits expire?

Unused credits stay valid for 12 months. Recurring daily campaigns use auto-topup so sending never pauses. You set the daily cap and can pause anytime from your dashboard.

Can I top up mid-campaign?

Yes. Buy more credits from your dashboard at any time. They're added instantly and active campaigns keep running.

Do you need access to my domain or inbox?

No. Distribute uses its own sending infrastructure. You don't need to touch your domain or connect a mailbox.

Are the emails GDPR and CAN-SPAM compliant?

Every email includes a working opt-out. Unsubscribes are processed automatically and the contact is permanently suppressed across all future campaigns. You remain the sender of record and are responsible for targeting prospects with a legitimate business purpose.
